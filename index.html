About

My name is Tony Graf and I have lived in and around Seattle my entire life. I am a graduate of the Web Development Certificate program at Seattle University. After being in my current industry for the last 10 years I decided I wanted to switch gears career wise.

I decided I wanted to get into Web Development because of the job opportunities, skills I will acquire and use, and the creativity I get to bring to the table. I have experience in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Vue.js, and Github. I am an enthusiastic web developer/designer looking to expand my experience and network.
